Who We Are

We believe African development can — and should — be different.

The Hive is more than a building. It's a living example of what happens when architecture, culture, and sustainability are fused with intention.

Located in Bijilo, The Gambia, The Hive was created to house more than businesses — it was built to hold values. It's a commercial space designed for collaboration, not competition. For community, not just contracts. For purpose, not just profit.

Why We Built The Hive

Because we believe buildings can shape belief.

Philosophy In Practice

When people walk into a space that reflects who they are — where the environment supports their growth, where culture is honored, and where community thrives — everything changes.

We built The Hive to offer a viable alternative to sterile, corporate towers. To create infrastructure that matches the energy of Africa's innovators.

To inspire a new kind of commercial development: culturally aware, environmentally conscious, economically empowering.

The traditional approach to African development has been to import solutions from elsewhere, creating spaces that feel disconnected from the people who use them.

We rejected this model entirely. Instead, we asked: What would happen if we built from our strengths, our values, our vision of what development could be?

"True progress doesn't erase identity — it elevates it."
— Affinity Development Philosophy

Our Three Pillars

Cultural Respect

Offer a viable alternative to sterile, corporate towers that ignore local identity.

Energy Match

Create infrastructure that matches the energy of Africa's innovators and entrepreneurs.

New Development

Inspire culturally aware, environmentally conscious, and economically empowering development.

This philosophy guides every decision we make — from the materials we choose to the companies we invite to join our community. The Hive isn't just a building; it's a statement about what Africa can build when we trust our own vision.

We believe that when African entrepreneurs have access to world-class infrastructure that also reflects their values, they can create solutions that the world has never seen before.

ROOTED IN VALUES

The Hive is a project by Affinity, a development company grounded in the African philosophy of Ubuntu — "I am because we are."

Affinity is dedicated to creating sustainable, culturally rooted solutions for African communities. Every brick we lay, every project we design, carries this core belief:

"True progress doesn't erase identity — it elevates it."

We believe that when development is rooted in local values and global vision, it creates spaces where communities can truly thrive and grow together.
