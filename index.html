Why The Hive

Not just an office.
A new kind of ecosystem.

Founded 2025 Bijilo, The Gambia

The Hive is The Gambia's first vertical commercial building rooted in African values, built to empower the next generation of businesses and ideas.

With 8 stories of eco-friendly design, flexible workspaces, and community-first architecture, it's a place where productivity, purpose, and people come together.

Whether you're growing a startup, managing a regional team, or leading a social impact mission — The Hive gives you the infrastructure, energy, and credibility to grow.

"Modern African architecture meets sustainable design"
